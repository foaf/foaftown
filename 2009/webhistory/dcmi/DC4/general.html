General Overview
This fourth workshop in the series will address major open issues concerning deployment of the Dublin Core and afford developers and planners an opportunity to share experiences with others.

Agenda items will include:

  • Extensibility Issues - The reason this is critical is to solidify the DC as the Core on which others may build to satisfy their own constraints. Without it, other groups will find some key element lacking, and will be compelled to start from scratch to reinvent the wheel.
  • Element Structure - Identification of default schemes and Subelement conventions.
  • Element Refinement - Semantics and clearer definitions for certain of the elements (for example, coverage, relation, and rights management).
Other issues may be discussed, but the major focus will be on the above crucial issues.
